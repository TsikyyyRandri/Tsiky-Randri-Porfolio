Les Jeux du Commerce bring together 13 universities each year for a competition focused on business pitch presentations, social challenges, and sports activities. Organized by category, the competition encourages students’ entrepreneurial mindset by challenging them to respond to real-world business scenarios. The event aims to develop critical thinking, public speaking skills, and teamwork.
