Vice President of Administrative Affairs Enactus Umoncton

Enactus Umoncton was an association founded by students on campus that aimed to support entrepreneurship among students. Our annual event, “Pitch-Nous Ça,” brought together 24 participants, 4 judges, 3 mentors, 6 business projects, and 1 grand prize winner who walked away with start-up funds.
With my Administrative Affairs team, we worked on coordinating activities and planning the event by communicating regularly with the teams and stakeholders. This experience gave me the opportunity to create a structured and collaborative environment through document management, writing summaries, and organizing logistics.

Teaching Literacy to children

CLEF is a reading support program that pairs volunteers with French-speaking elementary school children to help strengthen their literacy skills. I led and taught educational literacy sessions using PowerPoint and Google Meet. The 45-minute sessions (twice a week) focused mainly on grammar rules, followed by educational games presented in a simple way and adapted to different levels.
